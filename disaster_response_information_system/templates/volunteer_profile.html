Volunteer Guidelines

  • Safety First: Never put yourself in danger. Follow all safety instructions.
  • Communication: Keep your phone charged and with you at all times.
  • Reliability: Only accept assignments you can commit to completing.
  • Updates: Regularly update your availability status.
  • Teamwork: Coordinate with other volunteers and authorities.
